About

CampusChat is a communication platform that provides students, professors and staff members more robust communication and interaction. It is only compatible for Jacobs University, therefore only those who are officially registered under Jacobs University (whether that is as students, professors or staff) can access CampusChat.
This website was created and is operated by the CampusChat Group, which consist of Jasmine, Dragi, Fezile, and Dushan. We are a group of Computer Science majors at Jacobs University Bremen, who believe that CampusChat would make communication on campus easier and more professional.
